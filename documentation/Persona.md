# New User
## Persona 1:
- **Name:** Sven
- **Age:** 55
- **User Story:** 
- An old middle aged man that is a new user to the website but
  is familiar with current phone technology.
## **Profession:**
-He used to work, has a degree, has good assets saved up, but lost his white collar 
 job due to AI and is living off the government and some investment income. 
## **Technology:**
- Samsung Galaxy phone with an unlimited 5G data connection
## **Attitudes and behaviors:**
-Sven is a little disgruntled about the current white collar job losses due to AI, the high
rate of inflation, and decreased purchasing power of his dollar. He tries all behaviors
to reduce his spending and live within his means. Since his house is paid off, Eating costs are a big part 
of his monthly costs, so he's really interested in a software that can help keep costs of eating low.
## **Goals:**
- Sven would like to have an application which will quickly help her
  find a recipe based on what he has in the fridge. One of the main goal is that
  the recipe is easy to make and heathy.
## **Use-case:**
- Sven wants to know what he can make from the leftovers in his fridge.
## **Precondition:**
- Sven is at the page where she can upload a image of her food from the fridge.
## **Post Condition:**
- Svem was recommended 3 different recipes based on her image.
