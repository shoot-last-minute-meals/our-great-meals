# New User

## Persona 1 (Store Manager):
- **Name:** Natalie Brown
- **Age:** 52
- **User Story:** As a user, Natalie needs to find healthy and easy recipes to make based
on what she has at home.

## **Profession:** 
- Natalie is working in a grocery store as a manager so she is very busy.
When she comes home she does not want to think what to cook. She needs to get simple
recipe based on what she has at home at the moment.

## **Technology:**
- iPhone15 Pro with an unlimited 4G data connection

## **Attitudes and behaviors:**
- Natalie is busy. Very often she comes home after 8pm. She
has two kids and a husband. When she comes home she does not want to think what to cook.
Natalie is always looking for a recipes which are easy to make and healthy. Natalie
wants to make sure that her family has at least one hot homemade meal a day.

## **Frustrations and Needs:**
- When Natalie comes home after a long day at work she needs
to look to the fridge what is in there and based on that she is looking for a recipe
on the internet. This process is taking a very long time. She has some favorite
recipes, but she would like to get more inspiration - Natalie does not want to cook the
same meals over and over again. She is looking for an application which will help
her to quickly find the right recipe based .

## **Goals:**
- Natalie would like to have an application which will quickly help her 
find a recipe based on what she has in the fridge. One of the main goal is that
the recipe is easy to make and heathy.

## **Use-case:**
- Natalie's friend recommended her to go see website www.foodfromfridge.com where she
can find easy to make recipes based on a picture of food from your fridge.

## **Precondition:**
- Natalie is at the page where she can upload an image of her food from the fridge.

## **Post Condition:**
- Natalie was recommended 3 different recipes based on her image.

## **Interaction Flow (recipe from the image):**
- Natalie logs in
- Website shows her the general menu, option to upload the image, her top recipes, friends tab, and saved recipes tab
- Natalie uploads image of food from the fridge.
- Website returns the list of items it can see on the image
- Natalie confirms or add more items to the generated list of items
- Website gives her back top three recipes from her top three categories (top recommended recipes, Mexican food, Italian food)
- Natalie clicks on her favorite recipe
- Website gives her the list of all ingredients, step by step instruction to make the food, shows her the reviews, and an option to write the review for that recipe
- Natalie makes the meal, clicks done
- Website brings her back to the home page
- Natalie log out and goes to enjoy her meal with family

## **Interaction Flow (cook the meal from the saved recipe):**
- Natalie logs in
- Website shows her the general menu, option to upload the image, her top recipes, friends tab, and saved recipes tab
- Natalie clicks on saved recipes
- Website shows her a page with all her saved recipes
- Natalie clicks on one of the recipe
- Website shows her the recipe
- Natalie cooks the recipe and when she is done she clicks "I made it"
- Website brings her back to her home page
- Natalie logs out

## **Interaction Flow (Natalie wants to see what her friend is making today):**
- Natalie logs in
- Website shows her the general menu, option to upload the image, her top recipes, friends tab, and saved recipes tab
- Natalie clicks on "Friends"
- Website shows her a page where she can Add a Friend and see her friends
- Natalie clicks on "My Friends"
- Website shows her a page where she can see all her friends
- Natalie searches for a friend and clicks on their profile to see what they are cooking today
- Website shows Natalie's friend page where is also a section "Last cooked recipes"
- Natalie clicks on one of the recipes
- Website shows her the recipe
- Natalie follows the instructions to cook the meal and when Natalie is done she clicks on "I made it"
- Website brings Natalie to her home page
- Natalie logs out

## **Interaction Flow (Natalie wants to change her password):**
- Natalie logs in
- Website shows her the general menu, option to upload the image, her top recipes, friends tab, and saved recipes tab
- Natalie hover our "Account" and from the dropdown menu she clicks on "Settings"
- Website shows her a page with all the possible settings options
- Natalie scrolls down to the "Change Password" section, she enters her current password, new password, and confirms her new password. She clicks "Save changes"
- Website gives her confirmation that the password was updated
- Natalie logs out of the website

- Natalie logs in
- Website shows her the general menu, option to upload the image, her top recipes, friends tab, and saved recipes tab

- Website shows her a page where she can see all the recipes using pork meet
- Natalie scrolls through the recipes and when she sees one she likes she clicks on the recipe

- Natalie cooks the recipe and when she is done she clicks "I made it"
